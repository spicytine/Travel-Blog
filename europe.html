With a population of over 740 million, it is home to some of the world's most iconic cities, rich in art, architecture, and culinary traditions. Europe is steeped in centuries of history, with landmarks like the Acropolis in Greece, the Roman Colosseum, and the Eiffel Tower in Paris standing as testaments to its past.
