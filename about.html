How it all started.

Our travel blog began as a personal diary—born from a simple desire to remember our adventures. It all started after our very first backpacking trip, when we realized how many moments, details, and lessons we wanted to hold onto. We began jotting down stories of getting lost in unfamiliar streets, discovering hidden gems, and experiencing cultures that left a lasting impact. What started as a way to document our travels for friends and family soon turned into a passion project—sharing travel tips, itineraries, and inspiring others to explore the world with open eyes and curious hearts.
One unforgettable moment that changed our perspective was diving in the crystal-clear waters of Camiguin, Philippines, where vibrant coral reefs and marine life reminded us of the beauty beneath the surface. As we explored White Island, we felt the magic of Mindanao—untouched, diverse, and full of adventure waiting to be discovered. Wanderlust's vision is to empower travelers to explore the world with confidence and curiosity. What began as a personal diary has now grown into a space for dreamers, planners, and wanderers alike. We achieve this by providing insightful travel resources, budget-friendly advice, and personal travel stories that spark inspiration and connection. Our goal is to make travel more accessible, meaningful, and enriching for all—whether you're setting off on your first adventure or rediscovering the joy of the journey.

Our Favorite Destination

White Island, Camiguin, Philippines

A stunning, ever-changing sandbar with powdery white sand and turquoise waters, this little paradise is a must-visit. We love it for its unspoiled beauty, breathtaking sunrise views, and the dramatic backdrop of Mt. Hibok-Hibok. Whether you're here for a relaxing swim or simply to take in the serene surroundings, White Island is a true escape into nature.

Myeongdong, Seoul, South Korea

A bustling shopping and street food paradise in the heart of Seoul, Myeongdong is where K-beauty, fashion, and delicious local eats come together. We love it for its energy, neon-lit streets, and endless discoveries—from the latest skincare trends to sizzling tteokbokki and crispy hotteok. Whether you're a foodie, a shopaholic, or just soaking in the K-drama vibes, Myeongdong is a must-visit

Florence, Tuscany, Italy

This destination holds a special place for us because Florence embodies a perfect harmony of history, art, and culture. From exploring the renowned Uffizi Gallery to admiring the brilliance of Michelangelo’s David, every corner of the city feels like a living masterpiece. Its cobblestone streets, lively markets, and exquisite Italian cuisine create an atmosphere of timeless elegance that continues to captivate us
