Overview

Asia is the largest and most diverse continent on Earth, stretching from the icy tundras of Siberia to the tropical beaches of Southeast Asia. Home to over 4.6 billion people, Asia accounts for nearly 60% of the world's population. It is a land of contrasts, where ancient traditions coexist with cutting-edge modernity. The continent is rich in history, culture, and natural beauty, offering everything from towering mountains like the Himalayas to serene islands like Bali and the Maldives. Asia is the birthplace of major religions such as Hinduism, Buddhism, Islam, and Confucianism, and its landscapes are dotted with ancient temples, royal palaces, and historical sites.
