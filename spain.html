Spain, located on the Iberian Peninsula in southwestern Europe, is a vibrant tapestry of colorful traditions, world-renowned cuisine, stunning coastlines, and deep-rooted history. When is the best time to visit Spain?

The best time to visit Spain is during spring (April to June) and fall (September to November), when the weather is pleasant and tourist spots are less crowded. Summer (July to August) is popular for beach lovers, especially along the Mediterranean, though it can be quite hot inland. Winter (December to February) is ideal for exploring southern regions or enjoying snow sports in the Pyrenees or Sierra Nevada.

SPAIN'S CULTURAL ETIQUETTE AND CUSTOMS

Spaniards are warm, expressive, and value personal connections. Greetings usually involve a handshake or two cheek kisses among friends. Punctuality is appreciated but not always expected in casual settings. Mealtimes are late‚Äîlunch around 2 PM and dinner often after 9 PM. Tipping is not obligatory but is appreciated for good service. Dress modestly when visiting churches or religious sites. Respect the local pace of life, especially the siesta tradition in smaller towns where businesses may close in the afternoon. Joining local festivals like La Tomatina or Semana Santa with respect and curiosity is always welcome.
